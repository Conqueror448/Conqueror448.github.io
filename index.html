Services

Other services provided on request.

Custom Power BI & Excel Dashboards

Most businesses have the data they need—but it's buried in spreadsheets, scattered systems, or locked behind manual processes. I build clean, dynamic dashboards in Power BI and Excel that help you track performance, spot bottlenecks, and make faster decisions. Whether it's sales trends, labor efficiency, or production KPIs, I can automate your reporting and save hours each week for your team.

Logistics Efficiency & Inventory Tracking Tools

Lost inventory. Missed shipments. Stockouts and overstocks. These issues cost time and money. I build custom tools to track your inventory more accurately, reduce delays in your supply chain, and identify operational blind spots. From simple Excel interfaces to end-to-end Power BI solutions, I’ve helped manufacturing and logistics teams cut dock-to-stock time, boost inventory accuracy, and deliver on time.

ERP Data Cleanup & Workflow Optimization

Struggling with a clunky ERP system like AS400 or SAP? I help businesses clean up outdated workflows, eliminate data entry errors, and automate time-consuming processes. Whether you need better SQL reporting, a front-end Excel tool for your planners, or a process map that actually makes sense—I specialize in bridging the gap between messy data and lean operations.

About

With a background in manufacturing and process improvement, I’m driven by solving real-world problems through efficient, scalable solutions.

  • 2018-2023

    Modernizing Manufacturing at SABIC

    • Saved $500K+ in warehouse costs via smarter inventory controls
    • Reused waste materials internally, saving hundreds of thousands more
    • Built Excel macros for 100M+ kg/year production planning, reducing downtime
    • Supported SAP and analytics for a 1,800-person plant site

  • 2023–Present

    An Agency is Born

    • Cut shipment errors by 90% with Power BI and ERP integration
    • Spearheaded the enterprise rollout of a long-stalled sample tracking system
    • Improved lab and warehouse KPIs across sites using SQL, Power Query, and AS400
    • Became a cross-functional expert, bridging operations and analytics

  • The AI Era Is Here

    • As AI transforms operations, I’m leveraging tools like Power BI, SQL, and Power Automate to turn chaotic data into intelligent, action-ready insights. From real-time logistics tracking to predictive compliance, I focus on scalable solutions that evolve with tech
